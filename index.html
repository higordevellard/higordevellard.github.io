About

Welcome! I'm Higor Devellard

I graduated as an electrical engineer, but my fascination with the world of data analysis took hold of me from the very beginning. The moment I discovered the power of extracting valuable insights and information from data, and even utilizing machine learning models to forecast the future, I was captivated.

Driven by this newfound passion, I made the life-changing decision to transition from my career as an electrical engineer and fully immerse myself in the realm of data analysis. I embarked on a journey of continuous learning, devoting myself to acquiring new skills in programming languages, business acumen, and statistics. These skills were crucial in enabling me to effectively tackle complex problems in the vast domain of big data.
